==========================
Basic Device Drivers (BDD)
==========================

1 - Build them
2 - Place them inside Private/Root
3 - And continue your build.

===============
What are these?
===============

These are HCore device drivers.
Some of them are running in Ring-3, to let the driver restart in case of a crash.
